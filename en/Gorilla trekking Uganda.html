Gorilla Trekking in Uganda vs Rwanda

What Is Gorilla Trekking?

Gorilla trekking is a once-in-a-lifetime wildlife experience where travelers hike through African rainforests to spend one hour with endangered mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. Led by expert rangers, this regulated adventure supports conservation and local communities.

Gorilla Trekking in Uganda

  • Location: Bwindi Impenetrable & Mgahinga National Parks
  • Permit Cost: $800 USD (2025)
  • Experience: Adventurous hikes, 20+ habituated families, budget-friendly
  • Best For: Families, multi-park safaris, value travelers

Gorilla Trekking in Rwanda

  • Location: Volcanoes National Park
  • Permit Cost: $1,500 USD (2025)
  • Experience: Shorter treks, luxury lodges, near Kigali Airport
  • Best For: Luxury travelers, short itineraries, comfort-focused visits

Both countries offer unforgettable encounters with mountain gorillas. Choose Uganda for adventure and value, or Rwanda for convenience and premium service.
