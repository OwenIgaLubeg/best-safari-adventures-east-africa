Tanzania's most iconic national parks

Safari Details

Embark on a thrilling 15-day safari adventure through Tanzania's most iconic national parks: Tarangire, Lake Manyara, and the Ngorongoro Crater. This comprehensive circuit offers an unparalleled opportunity to witness the diverse wildlife and breathtaking landscapes that make Tanzania a premier safari destination.

From the vast savannahs teeming with elephants in Tarangire to the stunning views of the Ngorongoro Crater, this itinerary is designed for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Experience the rich culture of the Maasai people and enjoy unforgettable game drives, ensuring memories that will last a lifetime.


Safari highlights
  • Tarangire National Park: Home to large elephant herds and iconic baobab trees.
  • Lake Manyara National Park: Famous for tree-climbing lions and diverse birdlife.
  • Ngorongoro Crater: A UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning wildlife viewing.
  • Serengeti National Park: Witness the Great Migration and diverse wildlife.
  • Cultural Experiences: Interact with the Maasai people and visit local villages.

Safari Plans

  • Day 1Arrival in Arusha

    Morning: Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport. Meet your guide and transfer to your hotel in Arusha.

    • Afternoon: Relax and acclimatize. Optional visit to the Arusha Cultural Heritage Centre.
    • Evening: Welcome dinner and briefing about the safari ahead.
  • Day 2 Arusha to Tarangire National Park

    Morning: Depart for Tarangire National Park, known for its large elephant herds and iconic baobab trees.

    • Afternoon: Enjoy a packed lunch followed by an afternoon game drive. Look for lions, giraffes, and various bird species.
    • Evening: Check in at your accommodation near Tarangire. Enjoy dinner and share stories of the day.
  • Day 3Full Day in Tarangire National Park

    Morning: Early morning game drive to catch the wildlife at its most active.

    • Afternoon: Picnic lunch in the park, followed by more game viewing. Visit the Tarangire River, a vital water source for wildlife.
    • Evening: Return to your lodge for dinner and relaxation.
  • Day 4Tarangire to Lake Manyara National Park

    Morning: Depart Tarangire and head to Lake Manyara National Park, famous for its tree-climbing lions and diverse ecosystems.

    • Afternoon: Enjoy a game drive and a picnic lunch while observing the park's rich birdlife and wildlife.
    • Evening: Check in at your accommodation near Lake Manyara. Dinner and leisure time.
  • Day 5Full Day in Lake Manyara National Park

    Morning: Morning game drive to explore the park's varied habitats, including forests, grasslands, and the lake itself.

    • Afternoon: Visit the hot springs and enjoy a packed lunch in the park.
    • Evening: Return to your lodge for dinner and relaxation.
  • Day 6Lake Manyara to Ngorongoro Crater

    Morning: Depart for Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    • Afternoon: Descend into the crater for a game drive. Look for the Big Five and enjoy a picnic lunch in the crater.
    • Evening: Check in at your accommodation on the crater rim. Dinner and stunning sunset views.
  • Day 7Full Day in Ngorongoro Crater

    Morning: Another day in the crater for extensive game viewing. Explore different areas of the crater floor.

    • Afternoon: Enjoy a packed lunch while observing wildlife. Visit a Maasai village to learn about their culture.
    • Evening: Return to your lodge for dinner and relaxation.
  • Day 8Ngorongoro to Serengeti National Park

    Morning: Depart Ngorongoro and head to Serengeti National Park, famous for its vast plains and wildlife migration.

    • Afternoon: Arrive in Serengeti and enjoy a game drive en route to your accommodation.
    • Evening: Check in at your lodge/camp. Dinner and evening relaxation.
  • Day 9Full Day in Serengeti National Park

    Morning: Early morning game drive to witness the wildlife at dawn.

    • Afternoon: Picnic lunch in the park, followed by more game viewing.
    • Evening: Return to your lodge/camp for dinner and share stories of the day.
  • Day 10 Serengeti National Park

    Morning: Another full day of game drives in different areas of the Serengeti.

    • Afternoon: Visit the Seronera River area, known for its high concentration of wildlife.
    • Evening: Return to your accommodation for dinner and relaxation.
  • Day 11Serengeti to Lake Victoria

    Morning: Depart Serengeti and head towards Lake Victoria, the largest lake in Africa.

    • Afternoon: Arrive at your accommodation and enjoy lunch. Explore the local fishing village and learn about the culture.
    • Evening: Dinner and leisure time by the lake.
  • Day 12 Lake Victoria to Tarangire National Park

    Morning: Depart Lake Victoria and return to Tarangire National Park for more wildlife viewing.

    • Afternoon: Enjoy a packed lunch in the park followed by an afternoon game drive.
    • Evening: Check in at your accommodation near Tarangire. Dinner and relaxation.
  • Day 13 Tarangire to Arusha

    Morning: Final game drive in Tarangire before departing for Arusha.

    • Afternoon: Arrive in Arusha for lunch and leisure time. Optional visit to local markets or cultural sites.
    • Evening: Dinner at a local restaurant and overnight stay in Arusha.
  • Day 14Arusha to Kilimanjaro National Park

    Morning: Depart for Kilimanjaro National Park for a day hike. Enjoy stunning views of Africa's highest peak.

    • Afternoon: Picnic lunch in the park while enjoying the scenery.
    • Evening: Return to Arusha for dinner and relaxation.
  • Day 15Departure from Arusha

    Morning: Breakfast at the hotel. Last-minute shopping or relaxation.

    • Afternoon: Transfer to Kilimanjaro International Airport for your departure flight.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What's the best time of year for this safari?

    The prime seasons are: ✔ June-October (Dry season - best wildlife viewing) ✔ January-February (Calving season in Serengeti) ✔ November (Short rains, lush landscapes, fewer tourists) Note: April-May see heavy rains; some roads may become challenging.

  • How physically demanding is this safari?

    This is a moderate-intensity safari featuring: ✓ Game drives with vehicle travel (4-6 hours/day) ✓ Easy walking in Ngorongoro Crater and Tarangire ✓ Optional Kilimanjaro day hike (2-4 hours) Special Needs: We accommodate mobility concerns with 30-day notice.

  • What wildlife can we expect to see?

    You'll likely encounter all Big Five (lion, leopard, rhino, elephant, buffalo) plus: Tarangire: Largest elephant herds in Tanzania, Manyara: Famous tree-climbing lions, Ngorongoro: Highest density of predators in Africa, Serengeti: Great Migration (seasonal) & cheetahs

  • What vaccination requirements exist?

    Health essentials include: • Yellow Fever vaccination (mandatory) • Malaria prophylaxis (highly recommended) • COVID-19: Follow current Tanzania regulations • Routine vaccines (Tetanus, Hepatitis A/B) Note: We provide first-aid kits but recommend travel insurance.

  • Can we customize this itinerary?

    Popular add-ons include: ✓ Hot air balloon over Serengeti (+$550pp) ✓ Walking safari in Ngorongoro Conservation Area ✓ Zanzibar beach extension (3+ days) ✓ Maasai village homestay experience *Customization fees apply based on activities.
