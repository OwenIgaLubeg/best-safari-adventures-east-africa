Safari Details

Embark on an unforgettable 5-day safari that takes you to two of Uganda's most stunning national parks: Kidepo Valley National Park and Murchison Falls National Park. Frequently Asked Questions

  • What's the best time of day for wildlife viewing in these parks?

    Dawn (6-9AM) and late afternoon (3:30-6:30PM) are peak activity times. Kidepo's Narus Valley is exceptional at sunrise, while Murchison's delta region offers spectacular sunset views with elephants coming to drink.

  • How do the landscapes differ between Kidepo and Murchison?

    Kidepo features rugged mountains with savannah plains, offering dramatic backdrops for photography. Murchison has lush riverine forests along the Nile, open grasslands, and the spectacular Murchison Falls where the Nile forces through a 7m gap.

  • What special wildlife might we see that's unique to these parks?

    Kidepo hosts rare species like cheetahs, bat-eared foxes, and mountain reedbucks. Murchison is renowned for its Rothschild giraffes, chimpanzees (in Budongo Forest), and the highest concentration of Nile crocodiles in Africa.

  • Are walking safaris available in both parks?

    Yes! Kidepo offers exceptional guided bush walks among wildlife with armed rangers. Murchison provides chimpanzee trekking experiences and nature walks along the Nile's banks - though classic walking safaris are restricted to specific zones for safety.

  • What should we pack specifically for these park visits?

    Essentials include: binoculars, neutral-colored clothing (no bright colors/white), sturdy walking shoes, dust masks for dry-season game drives, high-SPF sunscreen, and quality camera equipment with zoom lenses (200mm+ recommended for optimal wildlife shots).
